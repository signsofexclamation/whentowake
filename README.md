# whentowake

<img align="right" src="/.github/resources/icon.png" height="128">

tells you the best time to wake up, based on when you are going to sleep.

humans sleep in cycles. waking up in the middle of one leaves you groggy. waking up at the end feels natural. *whentowake* does the math and gives you options so you can choose what fits your schedule.

## download

*whentowake* is available on the [app store](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6759986378).
